also remember about 

ICU =  International Components of Unicode Library !

The anatomy of an ICU locale string
<language>-<region>-u-<key>-<value>-<key>-<value>...
Example: en-US-u-kn-true-ks-level2

en-US —> the base language tag (BCP 47): language + optional region/script
-u- —> introduces Unicode Consortium-specific options
kn-true, ks-level2, etc. —> the "keyword" options tacked on after -u-
